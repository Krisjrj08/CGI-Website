RUPANSHI POLICY

CGI has always been prioritizing women's empowerment in their work culture.
Women empowerment is one of the sectors for which CGI has been working actively and vigorously since time immemorial.
our innovative efforts, we have taken various steps to empower women in society.
This time we have brought another innovative idea in execution to support all the women working in our CGI family.
ourWe have implemented the ‘Rupanshi Policy’ in our organization for all the female employees by providing them menstrual leave.
Under this policy our female employees are entitled to get the following benefits;

  • She can take consecutive two to three days leave during her menstrual cycle per month to take proper rest.
  • The leave will be unquestionable, and no further reasons or questions will be asked to her.
  • During this period no further responsibilities will be added to her shoulders.
  • For taking the menstrual leave, she will be needed to inform the respective mentor or managing person for the leave.
