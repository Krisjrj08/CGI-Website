IOT workshop

Meanwhile before lock-down, the CGI (with the support of CHANDIGARH UNIVERSITY) had organised an IoT Workshop to bring the students community, aspiring minds and emerging IT Professionals close to the latest and upcoming technologies. We were privileged to have the presence of the best industry experts, coaches and professionals as Guest speakers on the said topic.
This included:
a) Mr Harmesh Lal, Head (R&D) - TCIL-IT (Govt. of India)


b) Er Karan Arora, Founder & CEO, ITRONIX SOLUTION
c) Mr Atul Pathria, CEO & Founder - Quinji Tech
and others...


Er Geetika Pandey , Director (Confederation of Global Innovators (CGI), served as the Convener for the Workshop and added that,"The IoT mixed with data science can change the way we live." The audiences were glued to their seats with curiosity filled in their minds. And the result was one of the most interactive session based on IoT with lot of exciting and facts-led segments. The note of thanks was given by Mr. Nilesh Sinha (Chairman - CGI). At CGI, we are committed to always brings the best opportunities for the innovator's community.









Hack-A-Preneur

HACK-A-PRENEUR event in which more than 500 teams from across the world participated in this event to showcase their ideas to the investors, the majority of the candidate was from Russia, Japan, Israel, Canada & India. This event got 2nd position in terms of popularity and participation on “Hack-A-Preneur” a global virtual Hackathon is held to encourage and support the innovators of the world regardless of their technical expertise.


Participant showed their divergent ideas related to space technology, farming, medical & social welfare, etc. the best part of this event was its online platform which helped participants & investors to connect to each other from the comfort of their homes during worldwide lockdown situations and they got able to utilize their free time in such bigger platform buckets with lots of opportunities. The event happened in three rounds from 5 Apr’20, 12:00 AM IST to 23 Apr’20, 10:21 PM IST.
Registrations were live from date 5 Apr’20 till 13th Apr 2020. More than 500 teams registered in which 200+ international players.

Round 1: Pitching /Ideation (13 APR’20, 12:00 AM IST to 15 APR’20, 11:59 PM IST) A two-minute video presentation or a PPT describing the problem statement and the solution proposal. 500+ candidates shared their 350+ ideas on the portal in this 1st round.

Round 2: Proof of concept of the problem (16 APR’20,(12:00 AM IST) to 19 APR’20,(11:59 PM IST) The evaluation was done on the basis of the proof of the concept.

The organization made no barriers to IPR secrecy; provided there should be clarity about the solution and how to solve it. 98 applications were selected for this round after successfully convincing about solution concept. They were evaluated by the team of the jury which consisted of Mr. Rohit Tripathy (Ranchi Mall), Mr. Amardeep Kaushal (Corporate Consultant), Mrs. Kasturi Sinha (Life Coach & trainer) and Mrs. Asha Pandey (Ideapreneur).

Round 3: Business model (21 APR’20, 12:00 AM IST – 23 APR’20, 10:16 PM IST)



The outcome

The startups selected were:
P2F: Plastic waste management system VerbalEyes: Device to deal with patient ssuffering from neuron diseases C-4: Conserving water by atomising it GrabOfit: Providing customised products to consumers based on their requirements
ElectrineIngenum: Transforming conventional vehicles to run on electricity
LioDio: Development of an algorithm in the form of an app to monitor and manage loans
Educaffe: Promoting a new form of upskilling and re-skilling
AyushMahajan: Exoskeleton to assist in the movement of the paralysed
Robbies: A blockchain-based monitoring system for foodgrain

"We used social media channels, educational institutions, international forum, and all other global platforms to reach out to these young minds. We received participation from 500-plus entrepreneurs and innovators, and got some of the most innovative solutions across the domain, including areas like fintech, agritech, IT, blockchain, ecommerce, and healthcare (especially related to neurology),"
Amardeep says. The screened ideas will be made available on-demand to enterprises working to fight COVID-19.



Finally Top 10 applications were chosen as winnig teams to be connected with investors, VCs and other opportunities.
These winning teams are also offered free entries in all upcoming CGI events for next one year.
In the end, a Certification of Participation was given to all the participants.
